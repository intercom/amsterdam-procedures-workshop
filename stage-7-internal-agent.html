Advanced: Internal Agent

A conversational layer on top of Fin — talk to your data, manage your config, and build Procedures in natural language.

Internal Agent is a new way to work with Fin. Instead of navigating settings and dashboards, you can ask questions and give instructions in plain language. It's currently in closed beta, with Procedures support in active development.

Talk to your data

Ask questions about Fin's performance in natural language and get answers with simple visualisations — no need to pull a report.

  • Explore metrics across time, intent, and segment — "Why was resolution rate higher than usual yesterday?"
  • Drill into the conversations behind the numbers
  • Identify patterns driving resolution or escalation
  • Get actionable insight on how to improve Fin's configuration

Manage content

Audit and update Fin's configuration by asking questions rather than navigating multiple settings screens.

  • Audit your config — "What snippets are related to Billing?"
  • Make targeted or sweeping updates — "We're deprecating this feature. The details are in this doc."
  • Understand how your configuration shapes Fin's behaviour — "Why did Fin respond like this?"

Debug conversations

Go beyond what the conversation log shows and understand the reasoning behind Fin's responses.

  • Deep dive into conversations to understand the why behind Fin's responses
  • Get suggested changes to improve or optimise Fin's performance

Procedures In build

The capability most relevant to today — and the one coming next. Internal Agent will let you build and refine Procedures without going step-by-step through the UI.

  • Build out Procedures with natural language
  • Optimise and improve existing Procedures via conversation
  • Create and run simulations to test before publishing
Where this is heading The Procedure you built manually today — defining steps, wiring connectors, configuring branches — will eventually be something you can describe conversationally and have Internal Agent scaffold for you. The manual approach gives you the mental model; Internal Agent will make it faster.

Fin Procedures Workshop

Amsterdam · May 2026 · ~3.5 hours

Today you'll build a Fin Procedure from scratch — a structured workflow that lets Fin autonomously handle a customer request end-to-end. We'll use a refund request as our working example throughout the day.

A Procedure is different from Guidance. Guidance tells Fin what to say. A Procedure tells Fin what to do — look up data, make decisions, take actions — without a human in the loop.

What you'll build

A refund request handler. A customer messages in asking for a refund. By the end of the day, Fin will look up their account, retrieve recent orders, check eligibility, and either process the refund or explain why it can't be done — all automatically.

How the day works

You'll start with a thinking exercise — mapping your own process on paper before touching the product. Then you'll move into Intercom, wire up data connectors, build the procedure step by step, and finish by stress-testing it against different scenarios.

The final stage is open time in your own workspace to apply the same approach to a real use case from your team.

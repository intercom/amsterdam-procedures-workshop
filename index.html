Fin Procedures Workshop

A hands-on guide to building your first Fin Procedure — from standard operating procedure to live simulation.

How to use this guide Work through each stage in order. Some stages are exercises you'll complete away from your screen — others are step-by-step walkthroughs inside Intercom. Use the navigation at the bottom of each page to move forward.

Stages

  • 1
    Welcome & Overview
    What is a Fin Procedure? What are we building today?
    ~15 min
  • 2
    Exploration: Map Your SOP
    Paper exercise — document your standard operating procedure before touching the product.
    Offline exercise · ~30 min
  • 3
    Translate to a Fin Procedure
    How does your SOP map to Procedure steps? Anatomy of a Procedure.
    ~20 min
  • 4
    Data Connectors
    Explore the pre-built connectors in your shared workspace.
    ~25 min
  • 5
    Build the Procedure
    Step-by-step: create the procedure, wire up connectors, configure outcomes.
    ~45 min
  • 6
    Simulate & Stress Test
    Run three scenarios — happy path, ineligible order, order not found.
    ~30 min
  • 7
    Advanced: Internal Agent
    How Procedures work with Internal Agent. Optional bonus stage.
    Optional · ~20 min
  • 8
    Your Own Use Cases
    Switch to your own workspace and apply the process to a real SOP.
    Own workspace · ~30 min
